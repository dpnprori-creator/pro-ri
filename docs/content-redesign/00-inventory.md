1. **Missing H1 tags across all pages** — major SEO issue; pages use H2 as the primary heading
2. **No primary CTA on homepage** — no "Daftar Anggota" or "Hubungi Kami" button
3. **Susunan Pengurus page is empty** with placeholder text
4. **Tentang Kami relies entirely on images** — vision, mission, roadmap are not accessible as text
5. **No contact details** (address, phone, email, social media) on Kontak page
6. **Articles 2 and 3 are near-duplicates** of the same event
7. **No social proof anywhere** — no testimonials, member counts, partner logos, or media mentions
8. **No newsletter/email capture** on any page
9. **Only 3 blog posts** — content marketing in its infancy
10. **Daftar Anggota form has no benefit explanation** — just fields with no persuasive copy
